The Shawshank Redemption is a drama film based on the novella by Stephen King. It tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongfully imprisoned for murder, and his friendship with fellow inmate Red.
The Godfather chronicles the Corleone crime family under patriarch Vito Corleone, focusing on the transformation of his youngest son, Michael, from reluctant family outsider to ruthless mafia boss.
The Dark Knight follows Batman as he battles the Joker, a criminal mastermind who seeks to plunge Gotham City into anarchy.
Pulp Fiction intertwines multiple stories of crime and redemption in Los Angeles, featuring hitmen, a boxer, and a gangster's wife.
Inception explores dreams within dreams as a team of thieves infiltrates the subconscious to plant an idea into a target's mind.
